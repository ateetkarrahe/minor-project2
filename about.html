About CampusEats

Who We Are

CampusEats is an online food delivery platform created as a college project to simplify food ordering within the campus cafeteria.

Our Mission

To save students’ time by providing a fast, simple, and digital food ordering system inside the college.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy online ordering
  • Less waiting time
  • Affordable prices
  • Campus-only access
